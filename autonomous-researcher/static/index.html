About RAGentic.ai

Understanding the Technology Behind Autonomous Research

What is Agentic AI?

Agentic AI refers to AI systems that can autonomously plan, make decisions, and execute complex tasks without constant human intervention. Our system uses a multi-agent architecture where specialized AI agents work together:

  • Planner Agent - Breaks down topics into research sub-questions
  • Researcher Agent - Searches and retrieves relevant information
  • Analyzer Agent - Synthesizes findings into insights
  • Writer Agent - Compiles comprehensive reports

What is RAG?

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) enhances AI responses by combining:

  • Retrieval - Searches your custom documents using semantic search
  • Augmentation - Adds retrieved context to the AI's knowledge
  • Generation - Creates responses using both your documents and AI knowledge

Result: More accurate, document-backed research reports that combine your specific knowledge with the AI's general understanding.

Agentic AI + RAG = Powerful Research

Personal Document Research (RAG Enhancement)

Upload your own documents and ask questions directly. Our system uses RAG to search through your documents and provide accurate answers.

Step 1: Upload Documents

Go to the Documents page and upload your PDF, TXT, or MD files directly through the web interface

Step 2: Automatic Processing

The system automatically processes your documents: chunks them, creates embeddings, and indexes them in a vector database

Step 3: Ask Questions

Ask any question about your documents. The AI will search through your uploaded documents using semantic search and provide answers based on your content

Step 4: Delete When Done

After your research, you can delete all uploaded documents and session data for complete privacy and security

Note: The general research feature (Home page) uses the AI's general knowledge. The Document Research feature (Documents page) uses your uploaded documents for personalized answers.
